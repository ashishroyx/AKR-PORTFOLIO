This project blends aesthetic design, seamless interactivity, and structured content management to create a stage for every writer.

---

## 💡 What is CurtainVerse?

CurtainVerse is a **writer-centric publishing platform** with features tailored for storytelling and creative expression. Whether you're posting a heartfelt poem, an epic short story, or a script for the stage – CurtainVerse provides the tools and atmosphere to bring your words to life.

### 🧩 Core Capabilities:
- ✍️ Write, edit, and publish creative works (Poetry, Prose, Drama)
- 🧑‍🎤 Build a personalized author profile with bio and genres
- 🔖 Filter content by type, genre, and post date
- 📚 Browse and read works by fellow authors

---

## 🔧 Tech Stack & Features

- ✅ **React.js** – front-end rendering and component-based UI structure
- ✅ **Tailwind CSS** – responsive design system with custom styling flexibility
- ✅ **Sanity.io** – headless CMS for real-time, structured content management
- ✅ **Portable Text & Rich Editor** – flexible formatting for poems and dramatic content
- ✅ **GROQ Queries** – fast, customized data fetching from Sanity
- ✅ **Live Previews** – write and see updates in real-time
- ✅ **Dynamic Routing** – SEO-friendly URLs for posts and profiles
- ✅ **Author Profiles** – display work by user with linked author pages
- ✅ **Post Filtering** – by category, type, and mood (future-ready)

---

## 🎨 Design & UX Principles

CurtainVerse is meant to **feel like a reading nook and writing studio** in one. The UI emphasizes clarity, typography, and flow, with a tone of elegance and emotional resonance.

- 📐 Clean, spacious layouts with generous padding
- 📱 100% responsive – optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop
- ✨ Subtle hover animations, fade-ins, and transition effects
- 🔤 Soft serif/mono type choices to reflect literary themes
- 🌟 Focus on **content hierarchy**, so user works feel published and celebrated

---

## 🚀 Development Highlights

- 🧠 Built around **Sanity’s schema-driven backend**, allowing flexible post types (poem, story, play)
- ⚡ Used **GROQ filters** to dynamically load filtered data sets (e.g., genre-based)
- 🪶 Created modular components for **PostCard**, **ProfilePage**, **EditorView**, and more
- 🔗 Fully integrated author-to-content relationships within Sanity
- 🔄 Synchronized preview and draft modes for real-time editing
- 🔐 Custom logic for draft vs. published content visibility (for future role-based features)
